Introduction

Asiantex Odoo Integration Vision

Odoo is not just a recording application, it is the company's nervous system. At Asiantex, every fabric movement (Gray, Semi-Finish, Finish Good) must be physically and digitally validated so that financial reports (Profit & Loss & Balance Sheet) are formed automatically without excessive manual intervention.

Fig 1.1: Asiantex Inter-Departmental Integration Map

1. Purchase Order (PO)
Buying or Restocking Goods

WORKFLOW
1

Navigate to Purchase -> Orders -> Purchase Orders.

2

Click New.

3

Select the Vendor.

4

(Optional) Enter the Vendor Reference if there is a reference you want to add to the PO (e.g., saving the vendor's delivery note number, etc.).

5

Select the items you wish to buy using one of two options:

Click Add a product and type the item name.

Click Catalog, select your items, fill in the quantity, then click Back to Quotation.

6

Click Confirm Order.

7

Receiving Goods: Once the items are received, click the Receive Product button or the Receipt icon (box in the top center).

8

Click Validate if the items have been received.

9

Click the PO number in the top-left corner to return to the PO page.

10

Billing Process:

If you want to create a bill, click Create Bill.
Enter the Bill Date.
Click Confirm.
If the bill is paid, click Pay and then Create Payment.
If you want to print the bill, click Print.

2. Purchase Order (PO)
Fabric Processing (Maklon)

SPECIALIZED STEPS
1

Navigate to Purchase -> Orders -> Purchase Orders and click New.

2

Select Setia Busanatex as the Vendor.

3

Enter the Vendor Reference if there is a reference you want to add to the PO.

4

Select the item KAIN GREY using one of two options:

Click Add a product and type the item name.

Click Catalog, select the item, fill in the quantity, then click Back to Quotation.

5

Click Confirm Order.

6

Sending Materials: If the grey fabric has been sent from the shop to the maklon factory (Setia Busanatex), click the Resupply box (top center).

7

Click Validate if the items have been sent.

8

Click the PO number in the top-left corner to return to the PO page.

9

Receiving Finished Goods: If the maklon factory has sent back the dyed fabric, click the Receive Product button or the Receipt icon (top center).

10

Click Validate if the items have been received.

11

Click the PO number in the top-left corner to return to the PO page.

12

Service Fees: If the costs for dyeing services and chemical colors are known, add these products to the PO:

Select the item JASA CELUP & CHEMICAL WARNA via Add a product or Catalog.

Fill in the quantity and unit price for the fees.

13

Complete the Bill, Confirm, Pay, and Print process as outlined in the previous section.

3. Manufacturing Order (MO)
Manufacturing Goods

PRODUCTION STEPS
1

Navigate to Manufacturing -> Operations -> Manufacturing Orders.

2

Click New.

3

In the Product section, select the product to be made.

4

Enter the Quantity (how many items will be produced).

5

Ensure the Bill Of Material (BoM) is filled, matching the type of product being made.

6

Click Confirm.

7

In the Component Status section, ensure the status is Available (indicating all production materials are available). If "Not Available," it means items are missing.

8

In the Forecast Component section, you can see the availability status for each specific component.

9

In the Lot/Serial Number section, enter the production code. If not available yet, type the code and click Create in the Lot/Serial Number column.

10

Click Produce All to start production.

Note: Produce All can also be clicked even if components are not available in the system, but it will cause system stock to go negative.

11

Printing Labels:

On MO page: Click Gear icon (top left) -> Print -> Label with QR Code.

On main list: Check MO number(s), click Print button (top center), select Label with QR Code.

4. Sales Process
Selling Goods

SALES FLOW
1

Navigate to Sales -> Orders -> Orders.

2

Click New.

3

Enter the Customer. Type the customer name; if they don't exist, click Create in the column provided.

4

In the Pricelist section, select the price list for the product. Stock levels will follow the selected pricelist (Shop or Factory).

5

In the Order Lines section, click Catalog to see the product list.

6

Search for the product based on type/variant, click the item, and enter the quantity.

7

Click Back to Quotation in the top-left corner.

8

Click Confirm.

9

Stock Shortage Handling: If the SO contains items with no stock, a confirmation box will appear. You have two options:

Click Close, then edit the quantity to match available stock or delete out-of-stock items, then click Confirm again.

Click Confirm with Available Items Only to continue; the system creates a new SO for remaining items.

10

Click Delivery (top center).

11

Click Validate when the goods are ready to be sent.

12

Print the Surat Jalan (Delivery Order) or Packing List if required.

13

Click the SO number in the top-left to return to the SO page.

14

Invoicing Process:

Click Create Invoice -> Select Regular Invoice and click Create Invoice.
Click Confirm -> If the invoice is paid, click Pay -> Create Payment.
To print the invoice, click Print.

5. Inventory Replenishment
Restocking Shop from Factory

REPLENISHMENT STEPS
1

Navigate to Inventory -> Operations -> Replenishment.

2

Search/Type the product or variant to be restocked. Note the following columns:

On Hand: Current stock available in the shop.
Forecast: Projected stock (On Hand minus items to be sold/shipped).
Min & Max: Minimum and maximum stock limits for the shop.
To Order: Amount to be ordered (Max - On Hand).
3

Fill in the amount you want to order in the To Order column or leave the default.

4

Check the box for every item you want to order from the factory.

5

Click Replenish (top center) and select Order.

6

Receiving at Shop: Once the factory sends the goods, the shop must receive them in the system:

Navigate to Inventory -> Operations -> Receipts.

Find the receipt document, click it, and click Validate.

6. Factory Delivery to Shop

DELIVERY STEPS
1

Navigate to Inventory -> Operations -> Delivery.

2

Find the delivery document and click it.

3

Click Validate.

4

Print the Surat Jalan or Packing List if needed.

5

Inform the shop staff that the goods have been sent.

7. Scrap Process
Removing Items from Inventory

SCRAP WORKFLOW

Use this to remove goods from the system outside of normal transactions (e.g., the use of solar/fuel).

1

Navigate to Inventory -> Operations -> Scrap.

2

Click New.

3

Select the Product.

4

Enter the Quantity.

5

In the Scrap Location, select Virtual Locations/Internal Use.

6

Enter the Scrap Reason (reason for removal) if needed.

7

Click Validate.

8. Inventory Adjustment
Manual Quantity Updates

ADJUSTMENT STEPS

Use this to change system quantities manually without a transaction.

1

Navigate to Inventory -> Operations -> Physical Inventory.

2

Click New if the product isn't listed; otherwise, search for existing products.

3

Fill in the required details:

Location: Location of the goods.
Product: The product to adjust.
Lot/Serial Number: If product needs a code.
Counted Quantity: Actual physical count.
4

Click Apply All.

5

Enter the Inventory Reason for the adjustment.
